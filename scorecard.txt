What are some critical competencies to look for in a candidate?
Efficiency - able to produce significant output with minimal wasted effort 
Honesty/integrity - does not cut churners ethically 
Organization and planning - plans, organizes, schedules and budgets in an efficient productive manner. Focuses on key priorities
Aggressiveness - moves quickly and takes a forceful stand without being overly abrasive 
Follow-through on commitments - lives up to verbal and written agreements, regardless of personal cost 
Intelligence - learns quickly. Demonstrates ability to quickly and proficiently understand and absorb new information 
Analytical skill - able to structure and process qualitative or quantitative data and draw insightful conclusions 
Attention to detail 
Persistence  - demonstrates tenacity and willingness to go the distance to get something done 
Proactivity - acts without being told what to do 
Additional Qualities Include 
     
Ability to hire A players (for managers) - sources, selects and sells A Players to join a company
Ability to develop people (for managers) - coaches people in there current roles to improve performance, and prepares them for future roles.
Calm under pressure - maintains stable performance when under heavy pressure or stress 
Creativity / Innovation - generates new and innovative approaches to problems 
Work ethics - possesses a strong willingness to work hard and sometimes long hours to get the job done 
High standards - expects personal performance and team performance to be nothing short of the best 
Listening skills - lets others speak and seeks to understand their viewpoint
Openness to critics and ideas - often solicits feedback and reacts calmly to criticism or negative feedback 
Communication - speaks and writes clearly and articulately without being overly verbose or talkative.
